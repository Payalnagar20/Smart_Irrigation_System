Smart Irrigation System

About Our System

The Smart Irrigation System is an innovative solution for modern agriculture, designed to optimize water usage, enhance productivity, and reduce manual effort. Leveraging IoT technology, this system integrates sensors to monitor soil moisture, temperature, and humidity in real-time. It allows farmers to remotely control irrigation via an intuitive dashboard, ensuring crops receive the precise amount of water needed.

Our platform includes a user-friendly website where farmers can access real-time data, historical trends, and weather forecasts to make informed decisions. With features like automated water pumps and seamless integration with Firebase for data storage, the Smart Irrigation System represents the future of efficient farming.
